Transport phenomena (TD0943)

The course overviews the elementary physical and mathematical background of transport phenomena. The main bulk of covered material consists of the following topics
  1. Fuilds: Definitions and properties;
  2. Conservation of mass: Introduction, differential and integral forms;
  3. Conservation of momentum: Introduction, Cauchy and Navier-Stokes equations;
  4. Conservation of energy: Introduction, Bernoulli equations;
