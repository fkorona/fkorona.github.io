Transport phenomena (TD0943)

The course overviews the elementary physical and mathematical background of transport phenomena in seminar format. The main bulk of covered material consists of the following topics
  1. Fuilds: Definitions and properties;
  2. Conservation of mass: Differential and integral forms;
  3. Conservation of momentum: Cauchy and Navier-Stokes equations;
  4. Conservation of energy: Bernoulli equations;
